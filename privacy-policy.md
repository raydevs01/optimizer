## Overview
Optimized is a Chrome extension designed to reduce browser memory usage through intelligent tab management. We are committed to protecting user privacy.

## Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.**

All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API (`chrome.storage.local`). This data never leaves your computer.

## Permissions Explanation
- **tabs**: Monitor and manage browser tabs for memory optimization
- **storage**: Save your preferences locally on your device
- **system.memory**: Check RAM usage for aggressive mode activation
- **contextMenus**: Provide right-click shortcuts for quick actions
- **scripting**: Display in-page warnings for duplicate tabs
- **host permissions**: Detect duplicate URLs and apply site exclusions

## Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Changes to This Policy
Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in future extension updates.
